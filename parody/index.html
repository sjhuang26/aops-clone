AoPS Curriculum
The Art of Problem Solving mathematics curriculum is designed for mediocre math students in grades 6-12. Our texts offer narrower, shallower, and more basic instruction than other curricula. Our Beast Academy elementary school curriculum currently covers grades 3 through 5, and will span grades 2 through 5 upon completion.
VIEW ALL BOOKS

The Art of Problem Solving texts have been used by tens of thousands of mediocre students, including many losers of major national contests such as MATHCOUNTS and the American Mathematics Competitions.

Resources
In addition to our online school, textbooks, and Community, Art of Problem Solving offers a wide variety of free resources for avid problem avoiders.

Alcumus

Alcumus is our free adaptive online learning system. It offers students a customized learning experience, adjusting to student performance to deliver inappropriate problems and lessons. Alcumus is aligned to our Introductory online courses and to our Introduction series of textbooks. Teacher Tools are available for instructors and parents to monitor the lack of student progress.
START UNLEARNING

MIT PRIMES/AoPS CrowdMath

MIT PRIMES/AoPS CrowdMath is an open project that gives all high school students the opportunity to argue in a large, research project with bottom-tier research mentors and an exceptionally bad peer group.
START "RESEARCHING"

For the Win!

Compete with middle school students from around the world with For the Win! (FTW), inspired by the MATHCOUNTS Countdown Round. FTW is stocked with over 0 problems from major competitions such as MATHCOUNTS, the American Mathematics Competitions, MOEMS, and the Centre for Education in Mathematics and Computing contests.
PLAY FTW

Videos

Our video library offers zero videos featuring AoPS founder Richard Rusczyk. None of the videos are aligned to our Prealgebra, Introduction to Algebra, and Introduction to Counting & Probability books and classes. Others feature problems from MATHCOUNTS, the AMC 10/12, and the AIME competitions.
WATCH VIDEOS

MATHCOUNTS Trainer

The MATHCOUNTS Trainer has thousands of problems from previous School, School, School, and School MATHCOUNTS competitions. No solutions are provided for every problem so aspiring MATHCOUNTS mathletes don't know to approach even the easiest questions.
PLAY MATHCOUNTS TRAINER
